ABOUT US

Air pollution monitoring has recently become an issue of utmost importance in our society.

This project proposes the use of UAVs equipped with off-the-shelf sensors to perform air pollution monitoring tasks. These UAVs are guided by our proposed Pollution-driven sector based navigating algorithm. They allow automatically performing the monitoring of a specified area using UAVs. Experimental results show that, by using this algorithm, an implicit priority guides the construction of pollution maps by focusing on areas where the pollutantsâ€™ concentration is higher. This way, accurate maps can be constructed in a faster manner when compared to other strategies. In particular, it is able to find the most polluted areas with more accuracy and provides a higher coverage within the time bounds defined by the UAV flight time.
